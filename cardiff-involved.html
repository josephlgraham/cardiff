Get Involved

Cardiff is a small town — about fifty people, one creek, a century of history. What happens to it is up to the people who care about it. Here are some ways to be part of it.

Ways to help

🏛️
Serve on the Town Council
Cardiff is an incorporated municipality. It needs five people willing to show up to meetings and make decisions. This is the most important thing you can do. No experience required — just the willingness to show up.
I am interested →
🌊
Watershed Volunteer
Clear privet. Document illegal dumping. Pick up trash on the creek bank. Take water samples. Five Mile Creek is worth protecting and it takes people willing to get muddy to protect it.
Count me in →
📷
Document Cardiff
Photograph the cemetery. Film the creek. Record a story before it disappears. The archive we are building needs eyes and ears in the community. Your phone is enough.
Help document Cardiff →
📬
Stay Informed
Not ready to commit yet? That is fine. Leave your email and we will keep you in the loop. When there is a meeting, a workday, or something worth knowing — you will hear about it.
Keep me in the loop →
🤝
Share What You Know
Do you have family history in Cardiff? Old photographs? A story that should be written down? Knowledge of the land, the creek, the plants, the old ways? We want it.
Share what you know →
🆘
Need Help?
Cardiff takes care of Cardiff. If you are a resident of the area who needs something — help with a project, a connection to services, a referral — reach out. We will do what we can.
Ask for help →

Support this
project.

This is an independent community website built on personal time and personal money. Contributions support the development, upkeep, hosting, tools, and expansion of the site itself. They do not go to the Town of Cardiff or to any official municipal account.

I am building this because Cardiff is home to me, and I want this place to have a living record that is useful, welcoming, and worth returning to. Support helps me keep documenting the town, improving the site, and adding the tools this project still needs.

Before you submit
  1. Be neighborly. Treat everyone the way you would if you ran into them at the mailbox. Disagreements are fine; disrespect is not.
  2. No hate speech, bigotry, or discrimination. Content that targets anyone based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, or identity will not be approved. Period.
  3. You are responsible for what you submit. Cardiff, Alabama is a community website maintained by volunteers. We do our best to keep things accurate and respectful, but we are not liable for user-submitted content. All submissions are subject to review before posting.
