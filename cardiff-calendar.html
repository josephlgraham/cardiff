📅 Community Calendar

A place for Cardiff dates that matter.

This page is the public date spine for the site: season windows, frost timing, sky markers, workdays, and civic dates once they are truly set. It is meant to help people plan, not just fill space.

What belongs here
One calendar, shared across the site.
The Almanac and this page now read from the same seasonal list. That means new foraging windows, meteor dates, and civic markers only need to be added once to stay in sync.
  • Nature windows belong here when they help people plan a walk, a harvest, or a creek visit.
  • Sky dates belong here when they are worth stepping outside for.
  • Meetings belong here when they have a real posted date, not before.
  • Town updates can still hit the alert strip while bigger dates live here for the longer view.
