Some of these dates will look unfamiliar — Candlemas, Beltane, Lammas, Hallowtide. They were carried here by the families who built Cardiff: the Stewarts from Scotland, the Woodfords from Wales, the Tombrellos from Sicily, the Negrons from France. A lot of those names have been lost over the generations. The days themselves haven't.
